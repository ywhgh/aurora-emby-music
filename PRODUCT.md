# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who listen to a personal Emby music library on desktop and mobile browsers. They need quick access to music, playback, queue, quality settings, lyrics, and immersive playback without fighting the layout on small screens.

## Product Purpose

Aurora Emby Music is a responsive web music player for connecting to an Emby server, browsing a music library, and playing tracks with practical online quality controls. Success means login is stable, playback controls are always reachable, and the interface stays readable on mobile.

## Brand Personality

Clean, lively, focused. The product should feel light and polished, with a red music identity, subtle colored icon accents, and restrained motion that communicates playback state.

## Anti-references

Avoid dark-first layouts, crowded toolbars, oversized text in compact panels, hidden primary playback controls, generic blue-only styling, and documentation that names outside inspiration sources.

## Design Principles

- Keep playback reachable: core controls must remain visible without scrolling on mobile.
- Preserve the music identity: red remains the primary accent, with small colored icon details for orientation.
- Reduce small-screen density: collapse secondary actions into sheets and make lists easy to tap.
- Make state visible: playing, buffering, selected tracks, errors, and empty states should have clear feedback.
- Keep changes functional: visual polish must not break Emby login, library loading, playback, lyrics, queue, or quality selection.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target practical WCAG AA behavior for contrast, focus visibility, tap target size, reduced-motion support, and keyboard operation of forms, dialogs, lists, and playback controls.
